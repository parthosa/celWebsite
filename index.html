Center for Entrepreneurial Leadership

The Center for Entrepreneurial Leadership (CEL) is an entrepreneurial community at BITS Pilani. It was set up to create entrepreneurial leaders and have a significant impact on the global entrepreneurial community.
The CEL center is one of the top 5 Centres of Entrepreneurship among Indian Universities.[citation needed] It is one of the Centres for Excellence at BITS Pilani and also one of the founders of National Entrepreneurship Network (NEN).
