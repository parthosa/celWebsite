Conquest

About This Project

Conquest is an International Start-up Conclave organized by students at the Centre for Entrepreneurial Leadership (CEL), BITS Pilani. Having started in 2004, the competition is currently in its eleventh year of inception. Through an amalgamation of incubation opportunities, mentoring sessions and interactive workshops with industry stalwarts, Conquest, over its ten editions has served as a perfect launch pad for start-ups and has emerged as one of the top competitions of its type in the Indian subcontinent. The eleventh edition, Conquest 2015 took place in New Delhi on 6th September.


Success Stories

  • iTraveller (Runner up Conquest 2013) - Online cloud based travel discovery, planning and booking platform.

  • Solar Town (Runner up Conquest 2012) - Bangalore based, Clean energy solution provider.

  • Poster Gully (Finalist Conquest 2012) - Online fan Merchandise Company.

  • Phyzok (Winner Conquest 2011) - Technology driven classroom solutions; listed in top 10 start-ups by BusinessWorld in 2011.

  • GharPay (Runner up Conquest 2011) - Doorstep cash payment network for enterprises.

  • Mobile Medics (Winner Conquest 2005) - Provides primary healthcare in rural areas; Acquired by Piramal Healthcare in 2008.

  • iViz Security (Winner, 2006) - Provides Information Security solutions and automated Penetration testing.

  • Vita Beans Neural Solutions - leading technology provider in the field of assessments and behavioural profiling.

  • Vision Conquest, BITS Pilani was conceptualized in 2004 with the aim of inviting the most innovative start-ups, mentoring them and helping them network with potential investors.
